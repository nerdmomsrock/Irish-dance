St Patrick Is My Homie

We practice hard and play harder. Irish dance is about more than wearing a wig and a nice dress. We dedicate hours every week to stretching and drilling, so that what you see looks totally effortless!

We aren't cute little girls with pretty curls. We are Celtic Warriors, we dance in bars, at parks, and in gyms, from America to Ireland, all year 'round. But...our favorite time of year is St Patrick's Day, and in 2021, we plan to kick Covid's ass and not let it steal that from us again!

Our motto? "Go ahead, underestimate us. That will be fun." If you think it's easy, try doing THIS! At Clann Na Cara School of Irish Dance, we can and do keep up with the best of the best!
